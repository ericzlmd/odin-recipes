Red Garlic Pasta

  • 16oz rigatoni pasta
  • 1 clove fresh garlic
  • 2 fresh red bell pepper
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 1tbsp parsely, chopped, plus more garnish
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • black pepper to taste
  • 2 tsbp nutritional yeast
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes (if using) to the skillet and saut√© for about 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly, until the garlic is fragrant and lightly golden.
  4. Remove the skillet from heat and set it aside.
  5. Drain the cooked pasta and reserve about 1/2 cup of the pasta water.
  6. Add the pasta to the skillet with the garlic and olive oil.
  7. Toss the pasta with the garlic and oil until it is well coated.
  8. If the pasta seems dry, add a little bit of the reserved pasta water to the skillet to create a sauce.
  9. Season the pasta with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta and toss to combine.
  11. Serve the pasta hot, garnished with chopped parsley (if using).
